Who are the Unsaars?

The Unsaar are the local people who are willing to help their fellow brothers and sisters in the humanity. In this Refugees crisis time, they will be the first to help any Refugees and their family members who chose DFW as their new home. Unsaars will help them to settle down, get a job, and find a way to live in America, their new home.
